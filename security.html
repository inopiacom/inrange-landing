Security

Last updated: 2026-07-01

Our posture

  • NIS 2-aligned security operations (incident response, vulnerability disclosure, MFA).
  • EU-hosted infrastructure (Stockholm) with encrypted-at-rest storage.
  • TLS 1.3 for all network traffic.
  • Least-privilege access controls with audit logging.
  • Regular dependency audits (Renovate + pip-audit).

Report a vulnerability

Found something? Please email security@getinrange.ai. We follow a 90-day coordinated disclosure timeline and will acknowledge your report within 48 hours.

We don't offer paid bounties yet, but we credit reporters publicly (with your permission) and send swag for validated findings.
