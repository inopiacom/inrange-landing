Questions people ask

Is this a medical device?

No. InRange AI is a wellness and coaching product. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace your healthcare team. It doesn't recommend insulin doses. Always follow your doctor's guidance.

Which sensors do you support?

Dexcom (G6, G7), FreeStyle Libre (2, 3), Eversense, and manual fingerstick entry. Apple Health integration lets you pull in anything HealthKit knows about.

Where is my data stored?

EU (Stockholm) by default, US region available at launch. Encrypted at rest and in transit. Never sold to advertisers or third parties. You can delete everything with one tap and we mean it.

What does it cost?

$19.99/month (SEK 199 / £17.99 / €19.99) at launch. Founders Pass is a one-time $299 for lifetime access — capped at the first 500 sign-ups.

Android?

iOS first. Android is planned for 2027 based on how iOS goes. If you're on Android and want to be counted, join the waitlist with a note — it helps us prioritize.
